## Design Context

### Users
Personal users tracking dates and events, likely tech-savvy individuals who appreciate sleek interfaces. Used in various contexts - daytime planning on light mode, evening/night review on dark mode.

### Brand Personality
Precise, sophisticated, forward-thinking. The interface should feel like a well-crafted tool - confident without being cold, technical without being sterile. Think "thoughtful engineer's desk" not "corporate dashboard."

### Aesthetic Direction
Modern tech aesthetic with dual theme support (light/dark toggle). Clean geometric forms, subtle accent colors that pop against neutral backgrounds, crisp typography. Avoids generic AI patterns: no purple gradients, no glassmorphism, no sparklines, no gradient text. Inspired by premium developer tools and minimalist productivity apps.

### Design Principles
1. **Intentional minimalism** - Every pixel serves a purpose; whitespace creates clarity
2. **Theme-aware design** - Both light and dark modes feel equally polished with proper contrast and tinted neutrals
3. **Subtle sophistication** - Refined details (micro-interactions, precise spacing) over decorative flourishes
4. **Typography-driven hierarchy** - Distinctive font pairing with clear visual weight differentiation
5. **Restraint in color** - Neutral foundations with strategic accent use; accents work because they're rare
